Kids's Birthday Parties

Art & Soul has participated in over 10 kids' birthday parties.

Not only is art an amazing and fun thing to integrate with a birthday, but all the kids have so much fun creating together and being able to take something they made home is always the highlight of a great party!

We take the theme of the party into consideration of our art projects. For example, we did a 'cupcake themed' birthday party and the kids made amazing paintings of cupcakes and pasteries. No theme? No problem! We are pretty creative.

Adults' parties

Get a group of friends together for an unforgettable art and wine night! As art teachers, we are never short of art supplies and often find ourselves making art and having wine with our friends on our time off, it's always packed with laughter, fun, painting, learning new art skills. We can teach you and your crew through a lanscape painting, a still-life, maybe a self-portrait.
