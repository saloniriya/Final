Home School Art Class

We are happy to announce Art & Soul is now an approved Ocean Grove vendor, providing quality educational instruction to charter school students. Our current After School & Summer Camp programs equip pupils with the tools and openness to creatively explore their world and pursue a lifetime of curiosity and mindfulness.

We customize a program best fit for your personal requirements, while maintaining a well-rounded curriculum to ensure students reach their full potential. Our visual art program highlights world cultures, individual artist, their work and significance. Using a variety of media, our students experience a robust, creative process strengthen observational skills and develop drawing, sculpture and painting techniques in contemporary and classical styles.

• Students will examine Elements of Art and Principles of Design
• No projects are repeated throughout the year.
Visual arts and mindfulness teach children to make better judgments about qualitative relationships. We celebrate multiple perspectives and recognize the many ways to interpret the world.
